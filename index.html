My Poems

When it rains...

When it rains, I become a girl
running into a misty forest with a
lose hair, bare feet, a ball gown.

I become a girl with magic,
A Witch.
I become a spell caster,
Surrounded by vampires, blood suckers.

When it rains,
I become a girl in story of fiction.
Running away from the ball of suited
prince's rules and regulations.
Such unpreferred features.

When it rains,
I become a girl who dances without a partner
in a deep, dark forest
with the moon lit up at night, wet.
I am listening to pitter patters.

When it rains,
I'm awestrucked by the call of
Lightning and thunder.
A peaceful shower.
Swaying to the rhythm of trees and flowers.

But I'm here on my couch
Dreaming,
When it finally rained.

— Sujitha R
